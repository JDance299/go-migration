Organic Gardening Certificate

"Get a job you love, and you'll never work a day in your life". This quote has stayed with me throughout my career, and it stands as true today as it was 40-plus years ago. The incredible subject of horticulture, and the happiness it has given me, is why I am so pleased to support Garden Organic with its new organic growing online certificate.

We want this course to help shore up your passion for sustainable gardening, whether as a career or a pastime.

The unique thing about gardening is it's the perfect blend of physical, practical skill and science. As an apprentice and a student, I remember my week being mapped out as four days on the tools and one day in the class...
